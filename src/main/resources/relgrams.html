Rel-grams: Relational n-grams

A Rel-gram is a pair of open-domain relational tuples (T,T'). They are analogous to lexical n-grams.
  • Example 1: A person who gets arrested is typically charged with some activity.
      T = (police, arrest, X:[person]) T' =(X:[person], be charge with, [activity])

  • Example 2: A person who gets charged with some activity is followed by the person being sentenced to some time.
      T = (X:[person], be charge with, [activity]) T' = (X:[person], be sentence to, [time])
